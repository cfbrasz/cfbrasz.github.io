Swedish landscape paintings

I did all of these paintings based off of photographs of the Swedish summer camp (Brevik) I went to and worked at for many years. They're shown here in approximately the order I painted them in. The first half (11) were done in high school, another 6 were done in classes at Williams, and 4 others were done in free time in college or after (2 as wedding gifts). The black and white landscapes are prints from a printmaking class, and there's also an oil pastel landscape from high school. The rest were done using oil paints.
