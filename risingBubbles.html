Rising bubbles

Additional movies

Simulation (using Basilisk) of a 3 mm-diameter bubble rising in water beneath a wall at an angle of 22.5° from the vertical. Videos are rotated so wall appears vertical. Left movie shows vorticity in the plane z=0, right movie shows refinement level in the same plane and vortex cores found using the λ2 criterion.

Comparison of simulation and experiment at the same conditions, except experiments have a continuous stream of bubbles (flow rate 10 mL/min)
